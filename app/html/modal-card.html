With this Hand-Balance-Workshop we intense, but playfully confront our fears being upside down. By isolating the main-joints and body-parts in a structured way we are able to gain a better understanding of the positions. To be able to access the positions - we incorporate shoulder-mobility-exercises, and -theory. This builds the foundation, and can be integrated into your regular practice. So to get the best of your training after the workshop.

The mix of physical practice, experiencing, and understanding the role of different body parts while being upside-down leads to a good understanding and quick improvement. Gym Rings

In this workshop, you will learn how to use the “best upper body training tool” - the gymnastic rings. Impressive strength and abilities on the rings, such as the shoulder stand, the forward roll, and others, need to be well-built in order to efficiently and safely use the potential of the rings.

Softacro/mvmnt

In this Workshop, I present my concepts and approaches toward soft- acrobatics / acro-dance. We research and integrate continuity and agility in movement on one hand, and will focus on a selection of specific skills on the other. We drag apart elements from the areas of floorwork, acrobatics, the art of falling, dance and capoeira.

Some tasks are off abstract nature, so to leave room for interpretation and boost creativity, while others are clearly instructed, and look to improve a selected skill. Through the concept of isolation, integration, and improvisation even complex skills become easy to understand. We dismantle them like a puzzle, to be able to understand and integrate them afterward into the whole.
